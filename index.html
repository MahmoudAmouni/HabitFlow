About us

About Habit Flow

We believe tracking your health should feel like texting a friend, not filling a form. Habit Flow lets you dump any thought "walked 25 min, 2 coffees, slept at 01:30" and our AI turns it into clean data: steps, caffeine, sleep hours, mood, you name it. No menus, no calorie databases, just type and go.

Why we built it

Most apps drown you in buttons and guilt. We wanted something forgiving: a single box that listens, learns, and only nudges when it spots a pattern that matters to you. Whether you're chasing deeper sleep, lower caffeine, or just want proof you're moving more, Habit Flow keeps the focus on progress, not perfection
